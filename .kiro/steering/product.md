# Product: Traff✱k — Tokai High Traffic Simulator

An interactive, browser-based microscopic traffic simulation modelling the impact of ~800 additional school-run vehicles on the Bergvliet/Tokai road network during morning drop-off at the proposed Tokai High School site.

## Purpose
Civic advocacy tool — visualises congestion data from three independent models (Live IDM engine, SUMO Lab model, UXSim validation) to show residents and decision-makers what the TIA (Traffic Impact Assessment) numbers mean in practice.

## Core Features
- Live simulation using the Intelligent Driver Model (IDM) with rat-run logic
- Playback of pre-computed SUMO and UXSim scenario results
- Three demand scenarios: Low (500 trips), Medium (650), High (840)
- Four entry corridors: Firgrove Way (3A), Homestead Av (2A), Children's Way (2B), Main Rd (1A)
- Per-corridor stats: vehicles in/out, avg trip time, congestion score
- Road-level click-to-inspect via Leaflet map
- Editorial findings and model comparison sections

## Audience
General public / local residents — the UI is editorial in tone, not technical.
