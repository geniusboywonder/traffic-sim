Core Assumptions

TokaiSim utilizes the Intelligent Driver Model (IDM), a microscopic traffic flow engine where individual vehicle behavior is determined by distance and velocity differentials across the Bergvliet residential grid.

Inbound Main

Solid Circles: Arriving via primary arterial corridors (Main Rd, Firgrove).

Egress Main

Hollow Circles: Signify dispersal toward regional exit points (M3 Freeway, Ladies Mile).

Intelligence Reports

Corridor Saturation

Data indicates that residential corridor saturation occurs 18 minutes earlier than arterial models predict, indicating rapid local diversion during peak morning flow.

Recursive Queuing

Delays are primarily catalyzed by signal desynchronization at regional interchanges, creating secondary "Shadow Queues" across the neighborhood grid.
